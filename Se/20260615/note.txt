What is a Project ,
----------------------------
Project is 
    - sequence of tasks witch are carefully planned from begining to end
    - boundry by time, recources and required results

has a defined outcome and deliverbles
has a predefined deadline
has a predefined budget
conducted by a team of prefessionals

software development cycle

Planing -> Analysis -> Design -> Implementation -> Testing
