1. Identify why spotify exsists ?
- Back in the day we didnt had and way to stream music online, we had to download it and store it on our devices.
This was a problem because it took up a lot of space and we couldnt access our music from anywhere.
Spotify was created to solve this problem by allowing users to stream music online without having to download it.
